German sheperd

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Germany

Size & Weight:

Large, 50–90 lbs (22–40 kg)

Temperament:

Intelligent, loyal, courageous, confident

Exercise Needs:

High — needs regular training, play, and mental stimulation

Grooming Needs:

Moderate — sheds year-round, more heavily seasonally

Family Friendly:

Yes — excellent family and working dog, protective of loved ones

Fun Fact:

German Shepherds are one of the most popular police and military dogs due to their intelligence and trainability.

The German Shepherd is a versatile working dog known for its intelligence, trainability, and loyalty. Originally bred for herding and guarding sheep, they now excel in roles such as police work, search and rescue, service, and protection. They thrive in active homes with owners who can provide plenty of training, structure, and exercise.
