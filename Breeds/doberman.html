Doberman

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Germany

Size & Weight:

Large, 60–100 lbs (27–45 kg)

Temperament:

Alert, fearless, loyal

Exercise Needs:

High — needs regular activity and mental work

Grooming Needs:

Low — short, easy-care coat

Family Friendly:

Yes — protective and loving with family

Fun Fact:

Dobermans were originally bred as guard dogs and are known for their intelligence and loyalty.

The Doberman is a powerful and elegant breed, loved for its loyalty and protective nature. They excel in police, military, and service roles.
