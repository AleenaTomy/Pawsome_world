Origin:

Canada

Size & Weight:

Medium-Large, 55–80 lbs (25–36 kg)

Temperament:

Friendly, outgoing, eager to please

Exercise Needs:

High — loves swimming, retrieving, and play

Grooming Needs:

Low to moderate — regular brushing helps with shedding

Family Friendly:

Yes — excellent with kids and other pets

Fun Fact:

Labs have been the most popular breed in America for decades!

The Labrador Retriever is an energetic, loyal breed known for its friendly nature and love of water. They excel as family pets, therapy dogs, and service animals.
