Border Collie

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Scotland, United Kingdom

Size & Weight:

Medium, 30–45 lbs (14–20 kg)

Temperament:

Highly intelligent, energetic, hardworking, alert

Exercise Needs:

Very high — requires plenty of daily physical and mental stimulation

Grooming Needs:

Moderate — regular brushing needed, especially during shedding season

Family Friendly:

Yes — great with active families, loves having a job to do

Fun Fact:

Border Collies are considered one of the most intelligent dog breeds and are renowned for their exceptional herding abilities.

The Border Collie is a brilliant, athletic working dog bred for herding sheep in the rugged countryside of Scotland and northern England. Known for their intense focus and tireless energy, Border Collies excel in dog sports, obedience, and agility competitions. They thrive in homes where they get plenty of exercise and mental challenges — making them ideal companions for active owners who can match their lively spirit.
