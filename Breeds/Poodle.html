Poodle

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Germany / France

Size & Weight:

Varies — Toy, Miniature, Standard (5–70 lbs)

Temperament:

Intelligent, active, proud

Exercise Needs:

Moderate to high — loves activities and training

Grooming Needs:

High — needs regular professional grooming

Family Friendly:

Yes — great with kids and other pets

Fun Fact:

Poodles are one of the smartest breeds and were originally bred as water retrievers!

Poodles come in three sizes and are known for their elegance and brains. Their hypoallergenic coat makes them popular for allergy sufferers.
