Rottweiler

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Germany

Size & Weight:

Large, 80–135 lbs (36–61 kg)

Temperament:

Confident, fearless, loyal

Exercise Needs:

High — needs daily exercise and training

Grooming Needs:

Low — short coat is easy to maintain

Family Friendly:

Yes — protective and devoted to family

Fun Fact:

Rottweilers were once Roman drover dogs, moving cattle long distances!

The Rottweiler is a strong, intelligent working breed known for its loyalty and protective instincts. With proper training, they are excellent family guardians.
