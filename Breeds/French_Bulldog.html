French Bulldog

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

France

Size & Weight:

Small, 16–28 lbs (7–13 kg)

Temperament:

Playful, adaptable, affectionate

Exercise Needs:

Low — short daily walks and indoor play

Grooming Needs:

Low — easy-care coat

Family Friendly:

Yes — excellent city dog and companion

Fun Fact:

Frenchies are famous for their adorable bat-like ears!

The French Bulldog is a charming, sturdy little companion with a loving personality. They are perfect for apartments and are low-maintenance, fun-loving friends.
