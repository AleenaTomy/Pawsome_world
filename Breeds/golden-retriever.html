Golden Retriever

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Scotland, United Kingdom

Size & Weight:

Medium-Large, 55-75 lbs

Temperament:

Friendly, intelligent, devoted, and gentle

Exercise Needs:

High — enjoys daily walks, playtime, and swimming

Grooming Needs:

Moderate — requires regular brushing to manage shedding

Family Friendly:

Yes — great with kids and other pets

Fun Fact:

Golden Retrievers are often used as therapy and assistance dogs due to their friendly nature and intelligence.

The Golden Retriever is a beloved breed known for its friendly disposition and beautiful golden coat. They thrive in active households and are highly trainable, making them excellent companions for families, athletes, and service roles alike.
