Dalmatian

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Croatia

Size & Weight:

Medium, 45–70 lbs (20–32 kg)

Temperament:

Energetic, outgoing, intelligent

Exercise Needs:

High — loves running and play

Grooming Needs:

Low — short coat, but sheds regularly

Family Friendly:

Yes — great with active families

Fun Fact:

Dalmatians are famous for their history as carriage dogs and firehouse mascots.

The Dalmatian is instantly recognizable with its spotted coat and boundless energy. Known for their playful spirit, they thrive in active households.
