Yorkshire Terrier

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

England

Size & Weight:

Tiny, 4–7 lbs (2–3 kg)

Temperament:

Spunky, affectionate, brave

Exercise Needs:

Low to moderate — daily playtime

Grooming Needs:

High — long coat needs regular brushing

Family Friendly:

Yes — best with older kids

Fun Fact:

Despite their size, Yorkies were originally bred to hunt rats in textile mills!

The Yorkshire Terrier is a tiny but feisty companion with a long, silky coat. They are lively, loyal, and love attention.
