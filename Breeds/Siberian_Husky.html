Siberian Husky

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Siberia, Russia

Size & Weight:

Medium, 35–60 lbs (16–27 kg)

Temperament:

Outgoing, energetic, mischievous

Exercise Needs:

Very high — loves running and pulling

Grooming Needs:

Moderate — sheds heavily twice a year

Family Friendly:

Yes — friendly with everyone

Fun Fact:

Huskies were bred by the Chukchi people to pull sleds over long distances in harsh Arctic conditions.

The Siberian Husky is an athletic, fun-loving breed known for its striking blue eyes and friendly pack mentality. They need space and lots of activity.
