Pit Bull

Origin:

United States

Size & Weight:

Medium, 30–60 lbs (14–27 kg)

Temperament:

Affectionate, strong, loyal

Exercise Needs:

High — energetic and playful

Grooming Needs:

Low — short coat is easy to maintain

Family Friendly:

Yes — known for loving families when properly trained

Fun Fact:

Pit Bulls were once called “nanny dogs” for their loyal, gentle nature with children.

The Pit Bull is a strong, affectionate breed known for its loving personality and playful energy. They thrive with responsible owners and good training.
