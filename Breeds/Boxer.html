Boxer

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Germany

Size & Weight:

Medium-Large, 50–80 lbs (22–36 kg)

Temperament:

Playful, loyal, energetic

Exercise Needs:

High — needs daily play and walks

Grooming Needs:

Low — easy short coat

Family Friendly:

Yes — excellent with kids

Fun Fact:

Boxers are eternal puppies, often acting like silly clowns well into adulthood.

The Boxer is an affectionate guard dog known for its boundless energy and loyalty. They are patient, protective, and love to play.
