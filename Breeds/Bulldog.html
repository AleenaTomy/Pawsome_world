Bulldog

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

England

Size & Weight:

Medium, 40–50 lbs (18–23 kg)

Temperament:

Calm, courageous, affectionate

Exercise Needs:

Low — prefers short walks and lounging

Grooming Needs:

Low — occasional brushing and wrinkle care

Family Friendly:

Yes — very good with kids

Fun Fact:

The Bulldog is the national symbol of England and known for its distinct, adorable face!

The Bulldog is a sturdy, laid-back companion known for its lovable personality and unique appearance. Despite their tough look, they are gentle and devoted to family.
