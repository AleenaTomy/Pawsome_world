Beagle

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

United Kingdom

Size & Weight:

Small-Medium, 20–30 lbs (9–14 kg)

Temperament:

Curious, merry, friendly

Exercise Needs:

Moderate — enjoys walks and scent games

Grooming Needs:

Low — easy-care coat

Family Friendly:

Yes — great with children and other dogs

Fun Fact:

Beagles have one of the best-developed senses of smell among all dogs.

The Beagle is a playful scent hound loved for its gentle temperament and keen nose. They are popular family companions and are known for their musical howl!
