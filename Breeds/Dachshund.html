Dachshund

Home Breeds Gallery Quiz About Contact

Origin:

Germany

Size & Weight:

Small, 11–32 lbs (5–15 kg)

Temperament:

Playful, clever, brave

Exercise Needs:

Moderate — enjoys walks and playtime

Grooming Needs:

Low to moderate — depends on coat type

Family Friendly:

Yes — good with respectful kids

Fun Fact:

Dachshunds were bred to hunt badgers — their name means “badger dog” in German!

The Dachshund’s long body and big personality make it a favorite worldwide. They are lively, loyal, and full of character.
